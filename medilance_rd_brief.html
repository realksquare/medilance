MediLance

The Decentralized Healthcare Integrity Engine

Executive Summary

MediLance is a proactive intelligence layer designed to intercept healthcare insurance fraud before financial payouts occur. By embedding cryptographic trust directly into medical records at the point of origin, MediLance transforms the claim processing workflow from a reactive, slow audit process into a real time, high confidence verification system.

Our core philosophy: Fast track genuine claims with absolute confidence, and ensure legitimate patients are never the collateral damage of fraud.

The Problem Landscape

The current health insurance ecosystem suffers from a critical two sided failure. Fraudulent claims successfully drain resources, while legitimate claims face severe delays due to generalized suspicion.

  • Reactive Audits: Current systems rely on post settlement audits and rigid rule sets. These methods are too slow to stop fraud in time and too blunt, often catching genuine patients in the crossfire. (Source: Problem Statement Abstract)
  • Coordinated Deception: Fraud patterns such as duplicate billing, ghost procedures, and inflated diagnostics are only visible in aggregate over months, by which time funds are already lost. (Source: Problem Statement Detailed Explanation)

How MediLance Solves This

Instead of chasing stolen money, MediLance prevents the theft. We move verification upstream.

  • Tamper-Proof Provenance: Medical records, lab reports, and prescriptions are cryptographically hashed the moment they are created by the hospital. Any alteration breaks the seal.
  • Real-Time Interception: When a claim is submitted, the insurance agent scans the document. MediLance instantly verifies its origin, ensuring the hospital actually issued it, and checks the network for duplicate submissions.
  • Upstream Trust: By validating the exact origin of a document, insurers can fast track approvals for genuine patients instantly, eliminating the need for prolonged investigations.

Technology Stack and Components

MediLance is built for maximum privacy, speed, and institutional adoption.

  • Core Infrastructure: A high performance Node.js backend paired with a modern React frontend.
  • Decentralized Architecture: Designed to run locally within an institution's firewall. Hospitals and insurers maintain control over their own instances and user management (Master Admin portals), avoiding centralized data honeypots.
  • Dual-Layer Hashing Engine: Utilizes SHA-256 cryptographic hashing to create immutable fingerprints of medical documents. We employ both metadata anchoring (for standard records) and pixel perfect hashing (for scanned documents).
  • Identity Foundation: Strict role based access control separating Issuers (hospitals) and Verifiers (insurers), all managed by institutional administrators.

Competitive Advantage

Current solutions rely on analyzing claims after they enter the insurance pipeline. MediLance is fundamentally different because it secures the data before the claim is even filed.

  • Proactive vs Reactive: We stop fraud at the submission gate rather than trying to recover funds post payment.
  • Privacy by Design: We hash documents, we do not store patient health records. The system verifies authenticity using mathematical proofs, meaning patient privacy is never compromised.
  • Zero-Friction Integration: The system outputs standard QR codes on physical or digital reports. Providers do not need to overhaul their existing Electronic Health Record systems; they simply add the MediLance generation step to their export workflow.

Business Feasibility and Scalability

The decentralized nature of MediLance makes it highly scalable and economically feasible. Because each institution runs its own instance, there are no massive centralized server costs or complex cross hospital database integrations required. The system scales horizontally across networks.

Adoption is driven by direct financial incentive. Insurance companies adopt it to prevent millions in fraudulent payouts, while hospitals adopt it to guarantee their patients receive faster claim settlements, improving hospital patient relations.

Development Roadmap

Phase 1: Data Integrity Completed

Eliminating document level tampering at the source.

  • Established a professional, high authority user interface.
  • Implemented the Dual Layer Hashing engine for metadata and physical documents.
  • Built the Identity Foundation with isolated Issuer and Verifier profiles, governed by Master Admin dashboards.
  • Enabled hospitals to anchor records securely before they enter the claim cycle.

Phase 2: Fraud Intelligence Upcoming

Building the proactive risk scoring engine.

  • Duplicate Detection across multiple providers.
  • Billing Anomaly Engine comparing claim amounts against procedure averages.
  • Real Time Risk Scoring generating an Integrity Index for every verification.
  • Collision Alerts for simultaneous ghost procedures.

Phase 3: Network Analytics Upcoming

Visualizing coordinated fraud across provider networks.

  • Provider Risk Profiling to identify high anomaly rates.
  • Network Mapping to detect referral loops.
  • Ghost Procedure Filters for statistically improbable treatment combinations.

Phase 4: Fast-Track and Payout Upcoming

Using systemic confidence to speed up legitimate healthcare.

  • Express Approval routing for claims with high Integrity Scores.
  • Centralized Verifier Dashboard for intelligent claim triaging.
